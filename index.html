The “Choom Gang”

Barack Obama was “a pot-smoking innovator.”

He was in Hawaii's “Choom Gang.” Choom was slang for smoking marijuana. Barack and the gang smoked all over Hawaii.

Obama started a few trends:

  • T.A. (Total Absorption): Take too small a hit, and your next turn be skipped.
  • Roof Hits: After chooming in a car, breathe in the smoke pooled near the roof of the vehicle.
  • Interceptions: When a joint was going around, Obama would elbow his way into the circle, yell “Intercepted!” and take an extra hit.
“Thanks Tut, Gramps, Choom Gang, and Ray for all the good times.”

In his high school year book, Obama thanked his grandparents, the Gang, and Ray. Ray was “the older guy who hung around the Choom Gang, selling them pot”
